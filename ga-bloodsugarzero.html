Growing Around - Blood Sugar Zero

Script by John "Enter" Rozanski, art by Kamink.

[This episode starts in a mall, with chocolate fudge being poured onto a pile of french fries out of a ketchup dispenser.
The camera pans back to showcase Sally, doing this in a mall food court.
Autumn looks on, rather disgusted.]

Autumn: You're putting... chocolate on french fries?

Sally: I put chocolate on everything. I dunno why you're still surprised.

Autumn: I guess I'm just getting tired of all the Spooks & Kooks candy you've been devouring lately.

Sally: Hey, you know what happens when I don't eat my entire weight in chocolate every single day!

Autumn: Yeah, all too well. I think I still have bite marks from that camping trip.

[Robert and Max return with their food.]

Robert: C'mon, honey, we survived.

Max: Barely.

Autumn: Still, you might be overdoing the chocolate just a teensy tiny bit.

Sally: C'mon, Autumn, 'tis the season! What kind of kid would I be if I didn't—

[Sally pushes again and nothing comes out of the dispenser.]

Autumn: The kind of kid who's all out of chocolate.

Max: Sally... Spooks & Kooks Day was only three days ago.

Sally: Yeah, I know! I was being as sparing as possible with it! I'm out already?!

Robert: I'm sure you've got some more at home.

Sally: Yeah, if Gumdrops doesn't find my stash!

Robert: Well, someone around here is bound to have some kind of—

[Reverse shot to show Sally's seat empty, with nothing more than a cloud of dust.]

Robert: Chocolate.

[Sally slams her french fries down on the counter with a crash.
She is disheveled, like an addict.]

Sally: Do you have any chocolate?!

[The cashier kid looks at her.]

Cashier: Uh... yeah, of course. Just try and chill for a bit.

[Sally leans on the counter and grabs the cashier's collar.]

Sally: CHOCOLATE.

Cashier: Right away, ma'am.

[The cashier kid walks over to a chocolate dispenser.
He tries to pour it into a small french fry container.
He presses the plunger and a small drop comes out.
The cashier turns toward Sally.]

Cashier: Apparently we're all out of chocolate, so I'm going to say goodbye.

Sally: Why, you—

[Sally tries to jump at him but a security grate falls down.]

Cashier: Thank you for shopping at Silly Burger. I'm not really in the mood for dying today, so I have to say that our store is closed. Please come again soon.

[Sally mopes back to the table before crashing onto it.]

Max: I take it they were all out of chocolate.

[Sally pulls herself up weakly.]

Sally: Yes, and I don't know how long these fries are gonna—

[The camera pulls back to show that Sally's french fries are missing.
She looks to see that her food has fallen on the floor.
A janitor is sweeping it up.]

Autumn: Uh... Sally, are you gonna be okay?

Sally: Sure... as soon as I get my... choco... chocolate...
